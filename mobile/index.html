GREENSPACE STUDIO

ABOUT

Greenspace Studio is a landscape architecture studio based in Charlottesville, Virginia. We provide design oriented consulting, project management, and construction documents to clients both large and small. We focus on native and low impact landscaping, and and strive to poetically blend Doctor Seuss and the pragmatics of everyday life.
SERVICES

PHOTO TO PLAN

Developed as a budget conscious alternateve to the traditional landscape design process - the photo to plan service provides professional design services to the DIY homeowner.

Using pictures and a narrative supplied by you, and the answers to a few supplied questions, we develop a landscape design suited specifically to the aesthetic, intended usage, and budget of each project.
